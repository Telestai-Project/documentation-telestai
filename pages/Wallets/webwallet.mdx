# Telestai Web Wallet Documentation

## Overview

The [Telestai Web Wallet](https://webwallet.telestai.io/) is a **non-custodial**, **client-side** wallet that gives you full control over your Telestai (TLS) assets. It ensures your privacy, security, and autonomy by allowing you to manage your funds without relying on third parties.

## Key Features

- **Complete Ownership**: You are the only one who controls your private keys and funds.
- **Privacy Focused**: No personal data is collected or stored.
- **Cross-Device Access**: Use it on any device with a web browser, from desktops to mobile devices.
- **Integrated with Telestai**: Seamlessly interacts with Telestai’s decentralized ecosystem.
  
## Getting Started

1. **Visit** [Telestai Web Wallet](https://webwallet.telestai.io/).
2. **Create a new wallet** and securely store your seed phrase.
3. **Send and receive TLS** easily with the wallet’s intuitive interface.
4. **Recover your wallet** anytime by entering your seed phrase.

## Security

- **Client-Side Encryption**: All private keys and transactions are managed on your device.
- **Seed Phrase Recovery**: Your seed phrase is the only way to recover your wallet, so store it securely.
